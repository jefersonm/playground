- Paper Rock Scissors- 

Rules:

	Paper covers a Rock.      (Paper beats a Rock.)
	Scissors cut Paper.       (Scissors beat Paper.)
	A Rock smashes Scissors.  (A Rock beats Scissors.)
	Anything else is a "draw".
